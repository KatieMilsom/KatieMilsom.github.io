Warp Movies

LEFT: Model of the temporal variation of warps induced by a companion interior to the disc (orbit shown in red) using 1-d warp propagation theory. Only the inner disc is shown. RIGHT: Face-on image of the full disc, showing the shadows cast by this warp. A fast ray-tracing code was used to visualise the shadowing enabling us to rapidly scan parameter space for observational signatures that may match our images.
